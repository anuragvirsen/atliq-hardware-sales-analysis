# 📊 AtliQ Hardware – Sales Performance Analysis

> **Excel-based business intelligence project** analyzing AtliQ Hardware's sales performance, gross margin trends, and market targets across 23 countries (FY 2019–2021).
> Built as part of the **[Codebasics Data Analytics Course](https://codebasics.io/)**.

---

## 📌 Project Overview

AtliQ Hardware is a global hardware company selling products across 23+ countries through multiple customers and channels. This project dives deep into 3 years of business data to uncover growth patterns, profitability challenges, and product-level insights that can drive smarter business decisions.

**The core question:** *The business tripled in revenue — but was it actually getting healthier?*

---

## 🎯 Objectives

- Analyze net sales performance across customers, markets, and products (FY 2019–2021)
- Track Gross Margin (GM%) trends over time and across regions
- Evaluate market performance against set targets for FY 2021
- Identify top-performing and underperforming products
- Present findings through clean, interactive Excel dashboards

## 🔍 Key Insights

### 💰 Revenue Growth
| Metric | 2019 | 2020 | 2021 | Growth (2020→2021) |
|--------|------|------|------|--------------------|
| Net Sales | $87.5M | $196.7M | $598.9M | **+204.5%** |
| Total COGS | $51.2M | $123.4M | $380.7M | +208.6% |
| Gross Margin | $36.2M | $73.3M | $218.2M | +197.6% |
| **GM%** | **41.4%** | **37.3%** | **36.4%** | **-2.3%** |

> ⚠️ While revenue tripled, Gross Margin % has been steadily declining — a key concern for long-term profitability.

## 💡 Business Recommendations

Based on the analysis, here are key takeaways for AtliQ Hardware:

1. **Address the margin erosion** — GM% has dropped 5 points in 3 years. Cost structure needs a review.
2. **Revisit target-setting** — Missing targets across all 23 countries suggests targets may be misaligned with market realities.
3. **Double down on India & USA** — These two markets alone account for 40%+ of global revenue.
4. **Leverage breakout products** — AQ Mx NB and AQ Smash 2 saw explosive growth and deserve increased focus and inventory.
5. **Evaluate bottom performers** — Products like AQ Home Allin1 and AQ HOME Allin1 Gen 2 show very low quantities and may need repositioning or discontinuation.
